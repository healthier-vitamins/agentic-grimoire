---
name: grill-me
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, get grilled on their design, or mentions "grill me".
---

Interview me about this plan. Limit yourself to a maximum of 5–6 questions total. Prioritize by criticality — ask the most load-bearing questions first. Combine closely related sub-questions into a single compound question rather than asking them separately.

Ask the questions one at a time. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
